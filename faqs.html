Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Welcome to the BeaverRush FAQ! Below you'll find answers to the most common questions about how our platform works, what's allowed, and how to get the most fun out of your experience.

What is BeaverRush?
BeaverRush is a free-to-play social gaming platform designed for entertainment. We offer fun, casual games with virtual rewards — no real money, no gambling. Just good vibes and playful competition!
Can I win real money?
No. BeaverRush is strictly non-monetary. All coins, prizes, and rewards in the game are virtual and hold no real-world value. They’re designed purely for fun and progression.
How do I start playing?
You can start playing immediately — no downloads or payments required.
Is it free?
Yes! BeaverRush is 100% free to play. There are no entry fees or wagers. You may see optional in-game purchases for cosmetics or extras, but these are entirely up to you.
Can I play on mobile?
Absolutely. BeaverRush is optimized for both desktop and mobile browsers. Just visit beaverrush.com on your phone and start playing — no app store needed.
Is BeaverRush safe?
Yes. We take user safety seriously. Your account is protected, and we don’t ask for sensitive personal or financial information. We also use cookies to improve your experience (see our Cookie Policy).
What is the age requirement?
You must be 18+ or meet the minimum legal age in your country. By playing, you confirm you meet the age requirement. We promote responsible gameplay and recommend taking breaks if needed.
Is this gambling?
No. BeaverRush does not offer real-money gambling or any activities regulated as gambling. It's purely for entertainment and social interaction.

No Real-Money Gambling

BeaverRush is a free-to-play social gaming platform created purely for entertainment. There are no real-money wagers, no cash prizes, and no items of real-world value. This platform is intended for users aged 18 and over, or the minimum legal gaming age in your region. By using BeaverRush, you confirm that you meet these age requirements.
