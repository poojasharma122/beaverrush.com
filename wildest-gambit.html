How to Play Wildest Gambit

1. Game Basics

  • 5 reels × 3 rows, with 10 fixed paylines
  • Bet range: €0.10–€100 per spin
  • Medium‑ high volatility, RTP ~96.2%, max win up to 20,000× your bet

2. Symbols

  • Low-value: 10–A royals
  • Mid-value: compass, backpack, book
  • High-value: Rich, Cat & Gerard Wilde (up to 100× for 5 on a payline)
  • Wilds: appear on reels 2–4; substitute all except scatter
  • Scatter: Phylactery of Ra, triggers Free Spins

3. Location-Based Wild Features

Choose between Chile, Peru, India (unlock Egypt after triggering free spins in the first three). Each country offers different wild combos:

  • Chile:Multiplier Wilds (x2/x4/x8), with chance to expand if upgraded
  • Peru: Expanding Wilds, can become sticky with respins
  • India: Sticky Wilds + potential multipliers
  • Egypt (unlocked later): Combines all three features

4. Bonus Features

  • Free Spins: Land 3+ scatters to trigger 8 free spins + instant win (5×/25×/125× for 3/4/5 scatters)
    • A special expanding symbol is chosen at the start, triggering full-reel expansions anywhere on the grid
    • Active wild features depend on your current location

Quick Tips

  • Choose your preferred location to focus on a wild feature type
  • Unlock Egypt by triggering free spins in Chile, Peru, and India
  • In Free Spins, maximize the expanding symbol and combine wilds for big wins
