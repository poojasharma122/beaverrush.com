How to Play Tomb of Gold

1. Game Basics

  • 6 reels × 4 rows
  • 4,096 ways to win (left to right)
  • Bet from 0.10 to 100 per spin

2. Symbols

  • Low symbols: 10–A cards
  • High symbols: Scarab, Ankh, Pharaoh
  • Wilds: Replace symbols, pay up to 6×
  • Scatter (Sphinx): Triggers Free Spins
  • Gold Coins: Trigger bonus game

3. Bonus Features

  • Lock’n Gold Respins
    • Land 6+ coins to trigger
    • 3 respins, coins stick, reset on new coin
    • Coins reveal multipliers (up to ×100)
  • Free Spins
    • 3+ Sphinx symbols trigger spins (10–30)
    • Starts with multiplier (×1–×5)
    • Wilds increase multiplier, scatters add spins

4. Payout Info

  • High volatility (big wins, less often)
  • Max win: 7,000× your bet
  • RTP: ~96.29%
