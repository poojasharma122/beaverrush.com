1. Game Basics

  • Reels & Paylines: 5 reels × 3 rows with 20 fixed paylines

2. Symbols & Wins

  • Low-value symbols: Casino chip icons
  • High-value symbols: Fake Elvis, showgirl, oil tycoon, journalists, and an older couple
  • Wild (alien): Substitutes for all except scatters; can form walking wilds
  • Scatter (spaceship): Triggers Free Spins and pays anywhere

3. Bonus Features

  • Lock‑ On Respin:
    • If Reels 1 & 2 show a full stack of the same symbol (even without a win), Reel 3 respins while others lock.
  • Walking Wild:
    • If a stacked wild lands on Reel 3 during a respin, it becomes a walking wild that moves one reel per respin to the right; triggers an extra respin upon reaching the final reel.
  • Free Spins:
    • Landing 3+ scatters awards 12 free spins, flips the reel layout (reels reverse order), and switches walking-wild movement to the left.
    • Retrigger with 3 scatters for another 12 spins, up to 120 total spins.

4. RTP, Volatility & Max Win

  • RTP: Approximately 96.2%
  • Volatility: Medium
  • Max Win: Up to 1,000× your bet

Quick Gameplay Summary

  • Choose your bet ($0.01–$100) and spin.
  • Watch for stacked reels to trigger respins and walking wilds.
  • Land 3+ spaceship scatters to start Free Spins with flipped reels and wilds moving left.
  • Collect more scatters to extend Free Spins up to 120.
